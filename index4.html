The Guide to Surviving Anxiety-College Edition

Did you know that 63% of college students in the US felt overwhelming anxiety in the past year? Based off of my recent experiences as a college student, I think that the constant social and academic pressures has led students to face sever anxiety disorder, which is a problem that needs to be addressed

That being said, I hope to educate individuals on the main factors that cause generalized anxiety disorder amongst college students. Use this page to looks at facts, find resources, and understand the underlying effects of anxiety on these adolescents.
